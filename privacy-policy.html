Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use Grammar Check Pro, the text you select for grammar checking is temporarily processed to provide writing suggestions and corrections.

Technical Information

We may collect anonymous technical information such as:

  • Chrome extension usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Error reports to improve our service
  • Extension version and Chrome browser information

üîê Important: Your Writing Stays Private

We do NOT store, save, or retain any of your writing content. All text analysis happens locally in your browser and is immediately discarded after providing suggestions.

How We Use Your Information

Grammar and Writing Analysis

The text you select is temporarily sent to our AI service (Pollinations.AI) for analysis. This processing includes:

  • Grammar error detection
  • Spelling corrections
  • Style and clarity suggestions
  • Writing quality scoring

Service Improvement

Anonymized usage data helps us:

  • Improve extension performance
  • Fix bugs and issues
  • Add new features
  • Understand user needs

Data Security

Local Processing

Your writing content is processed locally in your browser whenever possible. When AI analysis is needed, data is sent securely to our service and immediately discarded after processing.

Secure Transmission

All data transmitted to our AI service uses HTTPS encryption. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information during transit.

No Data Retention

We do not store your writing content on our servers. Text is analyzed in real-time and immediately forgotten.

Third-Party Services

Pollinations.AI

We use Pollinations.AI for AI-powered text analysis. Their privacy policy applies to the technical processing of your text. However, we ensure that:

  • No text is stored by Pollinations.AI
  • Data is processed only for grammar analysis
  • Results are returned immediately and discarded

Chrome Web Store

The extension is distributed through the Chrome Web Store. Google may collect usage statistics for the extension, but this is subject to Google's privacy policy.

Your Rights and Choices

Opt-Out

You can disable Grammar Check Pro at any time by:

  • Clicking the extension icon and toggling it off
  • Removing the extension from Chrome
  • Disabling it in Chrome settings

Data Control

You have full control over what text is analyzed. Only text you explicitly select will be processed.

Children's Privacy

Grammar Check Pro is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
